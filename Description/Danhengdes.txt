Basic attack: Cloudlancer Art: North Wind (Q)
Deals Physical DMG equal to 100% of Dan Heng's ATK to a single enemy.

Skill: Cloudlancer Art: Torrent (E)
Deals Physical DMG equal to 260% of Dan Heng's ATK to a single enemy.
On a CRIT Hit, there is a 100% base chance to reduce the target's
SPD by 12% for 2 turn(s).

Ultimate: Ethereal Dream (100 energy cost)
Deals Wind DMG equal to 400% of Dan Heng's ATK to a single enemy.
If the enemy is Slowed, the Ultimate's DMG multiplier increases by 120%.
