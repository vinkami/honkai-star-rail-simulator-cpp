Basic attack: Monodrama (Q)
Deals Physical DMG equal to 100% of Sparkle's ATK to a single
target enemy.

Skill: Dreamdiver (E)
Increases the CRIT DMG of a single target ally by 24% of
Sparkle's CRIT DMG plus 45%, lasting for 1 turn(s). And at the
same time, Advances Forward this ally's action by 50%.
When Sparkle uses this ability on herself, the Action Advance
effect will not trigger.

Ultimate: The Hero with a Thousand Faces (110 energy cost)
Recovers 4 Skill Points for the team and grants all allies
Cipher. For allies with Cipher, each stack of the DMG Boost
effect provided by Sparkle's Talent additionally increases
by 10%, lasting for 2 turns.
