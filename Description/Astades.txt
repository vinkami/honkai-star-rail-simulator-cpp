Basic attack: Spectrum Beam (Q)
Deals Physical DMG equal to 100% of Asta's ATK to a single enemy.

Skill: Meteor Storm (E)
Deals Physical DMG equal to 50% of Asta's ATK to a single enemy and
further deals DMG for 4 extra times, with each time dealing Physical
DMG equal to 50% of Asta's ATK to a random enemy.

Ultimate: Astral Blessing (120 energy cost)
Increases SPD of all allies by 50 for 2 turn(s).
